**Note**: XAPP AI provides a hosted landing page for your business. You can share it on social media, emails, and from your website to let users ask questions, provide contact information, and schedule appointments


Your landing page can be configured with your specific settings including... 
- Your brand colors and business logo
- Your Business website
- Your Business address
- A link to your Business website
- A Contact or Scheduling from **Based on your subscription plan**
- Chat for answering questions and live support

## Prerequisites

- Your unique XAPP `link` to redirect to when a button is clicked.

## Getting the link

1. **Log Into Your XAPP Account**

   - Sign in and navigate to your XAPP dashboard.
   - Navigate to 'Channels'
   - Select Booking Widget
   - Copy the link

## Adding a button to your Facebook Page

1. **Log Into Your Facebook Account**

   - Sign in and navigate to your Page.

2. **Edit Action Button on Facebook Page**

    - Navigate to the page where you want to add the button.
    - **From the Facebook page you manage, click the 3 horizontal dots to edit your Action Button.**
